Mtandao Point

Affordable, Decentralized Internet and Digital Identity for Rural Tanzania

Mtandao Point delivers solar-powered Wi-Fi, blockchain-based digital identity, and mobile financial access to underserved rural communities in Tanzania — enabling citizens to access services, learn, and grow financially.

Community-Powered Mesh Network

Solar-powered mesh Wi-Fi with TP-Link routers (EAP660, CPE710) is deployed to provide decentralized and resilient connectivity in rural areas.

Blockchain-Based Digital Identity

With Hyperledger Fabric, we provide verifiable digital identities accessible through mobile devices, allowing secure access to services.

Financial Inclusion

Link digital IDs with platforms like M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and NMB to enable account creation, payments, and microloans for rural users.

How We Work with Communities

  • Start with Community Engagement: We collaborate with local leaders, schools, and groups to understand needs, raise awareness, and build trust.
  • Deploy Solar-Powered Mesh Networks: We install Wi-Fi stations using mesh topology, which connects multiple access points to create strong, flexible, and community-managed internet coverage.
  • Train Local Youth: We hire and train young people from the community to install, maintain, and support the network—building skills and creating local jobs.
  • Offer Digital Identity & Financial Tools: Through our platform, people can create secure digital IDs and access mobile banking, government services, and economic opportunities.
  • Support Inclusion & Literacy: We run local workshops on digital skills, financial literacy, and internet safety—especially for women, youth, and small businesses.
  • Partner for Impact: We work with banks, telecoms, NGOs, and innovators to connect services and scale sustainably.

Risks & Mitigation

  • Cybersecurity: Blockchain & AI monitoring + encryption
  • Regulatory: TCRA, NIDA & UCSAF alignment early on
  • Funding: PPPs + grant diversification + local ownership
  • Infrastructure Failures: Solar backups + youth-trained maintenance
  • Adoption: Strong local engagement, awareness, and champions
